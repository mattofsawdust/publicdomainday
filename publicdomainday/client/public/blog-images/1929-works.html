Notable 1929 Works Entering the Public Domain in 2025

The Sound and the Fury by William Faulkner

A modernist novel exploring the decline of the Compson family in the American South.

A Farewell to Arms by Ernest Hemingway

A novel set during World War I, depicting a tragic romance between an American ambulance driver and a British nurse.

The Broadway Melody (1929 Film)

Hollywood's first all-talking musical, winner of the Academy Award for Best Picture.

The Cocoanuts (1929 Film)

The Marx Brothers' first feature film, a musical comedy set in a Florida hotel.

Blackmail (1929 Film)

Alfred Hitchcock's first sound film, a British thriller about a woman who kills a man in self-defense.

Pandora's Box (1929 Film)

A German silent film starring Louise Brooks as Lulu, whose uninhibited nature leads to her downfall.

The Skeleton Dance (1929 Animated Short)

A Walt Disney Silly Symphony featuring dancing skeletons, iconic for its early synchronized sound.

Popeye by E.C. Segar (Comic Character)

The famous spinach-eating sailor first appeared in the Thimble Theatre comic strip.

Tintin by Hergé (Comic Series)

The adventures of young Belgian reporter Tintin began with "Tintin in the Land of the Soviets."

The Treachery of Images by René Magritte

Famous surrealist painting challenging perceptions of reality with the caption "Ceci n'est pas une pipe."
